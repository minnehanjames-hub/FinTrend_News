Sunday Macro & Positioning Note — December 14

Written by FinTrend News · Sunday Macro · December 14

Heading into the final full trading weeks of the year, the macro backdrop remains supportive — but positioning is doing more of the heavy lifting than fundamentals alone.

The macro frame

Markets continue to price a soft-landing outcome: growth slows but holds, inflation cools gradually, and policy stays patient. That narrative supports risk — until something challenges it.

Where risk lives

  • Rates sensitivity: Any upside surprise in yields could unwind crowded longs.
  • Low volatility: Calm conditions can flip quickly in thin December liquidity.
  • Breadth: Narrow leadership makes rallies more fragile.

Week-ahead mindset

This is not the environment to chase. Let levels come to you, keep sizing disciplined, and respect that year-end moves can exaggerate both winners and losers.
