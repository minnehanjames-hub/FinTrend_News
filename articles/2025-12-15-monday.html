Monday Market Outlook — December 15

Written by FinTrend News · Monday Outlook · December 15

The week opens with markets still leaning risk-on, but with far less room for complacency. Rates remain the primary lever — and December liquidity can amplify even modest surprises.

Core theme

The path higher is still open, but it’s narrow. Equities need stable yields and improving breadth to extend gains without added volatility.

What to watch

  • 10Y yields: The clearest risk-on / risk-off signal.
  • Volatility: Any repricing from low levels deserves respect.
  • Market breadth: Broad participation signals durability.

Trading mindset

Stay flexible, trade levels, and avoid forcing exposure. December rewards patience and punishes overconfidence.
