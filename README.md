# FinTrend News

## In plain terms

FinTrend News is a market-recap publication covering important moves in North American markets. New recaps post **four times a week — Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Sunday** — each as a standalone article page, with a browsable archive of past issues. The site also includes a set of free, downloadable finance spreadsheets to help readers act on what they learn.

## What's here

- **120+ recap articles** (`articles/`) covering NA market movements
- An **archive** page to browse past issues
- **Free Excel tools:**
  - 📊 DCA (Dollar-Cost Averaging) Planner
  - ✅ Earnings Checklist
  - 📈 Portfolio Tracker
  - 🔁 Rebalancing Checklist

## Tech

Static HTML site served on a custom domain (see `CNAME`), no build step required.

## Disclaimer

FinTrend News is for information and education only. Nothing here is investment advice.
